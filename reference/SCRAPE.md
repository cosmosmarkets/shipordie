## FAQ

### What app can I work on?+

Anything, as long as it can be shared with a link. SaaS, tool, directory, marketplace, AI wrapper, cursed little experiment. If the crew can click it, it counts.

### What if I already have an idea?+

Good. Bring it aboard and stop polishing the treasure map. The point is to ship the smallest version people can actually use.

### What if I do not have an idea yet?+

You will pick one fast. No hiding in the fog for three weeks asking the wind for permission.

### Do I need to be a technical founder?+

No. You need enough grit to get a link live. Code it, vibe code it, duct-tape it. The sea does not care how the boat floats.

### When does the 30-day timer start?+

After checkout, you choose the project you are committing to. Once that mission is created, the 30-day timer starts. After you ship and stay on board, you do not have to start another mission right away. Keep working on your startup if it is taking off. Start a new mission whenever you want, and that starts a new 30-day timer.

### What happens if I do not launch in 30 days?+

You get marked overboard, lose community access, and become a warning tale for the next batch of pirates who thought they were special.

### Can I change my idea after joining?+

Yes, early and for a real reason. But once you've signed the oath, you're committed to the idea and the 30-day clock.

### Is this a course?+

No. Courses let you feel productive while staying hidden. This is a deadline, an exclusive community, and a public launch waiting to judge your excuses.

### How much time do I need each week?+

Enough to move the ship daily. Some pirates have full-time jobs, kids, chaos, and bad Wi-Fi. The rule is progress, not perfect conditions.

### Will people help me?+

Aye. You get the crew, feedback, pressure, and visibility. You still have to swing the hammer yourself.

### Can I get a refund?+

If you genuinely asked this, blink twice and start over.
